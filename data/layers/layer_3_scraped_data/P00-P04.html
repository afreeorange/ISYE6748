Newborn affected by maternal factors and by complications of pregnancy, labor, and delivery P00-P04

Note
  • These codes are for use when the listed maternal conditions are specified as the cause of confirmed morbidity or potential morbidity which have their origin in the perinatal period (before birth through the first 28 days after birth).
Codes
  • P00
    Newborn affected by maternal conditions that may be unrelated to present pregnancy
  • P01
    Newborn affected by maternal complications of pregnancy
  • P02
    Newborn affected by complications of placenta, cord and membranes
  • P03
    Newborn affected by other complications of labor and delivery
  • P04
    Newborn affected by noxious substances transmitted via placenta or breast milk
